- GA is relying almost entirely on DeBERTa and using the five heuristic features as very small penalty-style nudges.
- Among heuristics, concreteness and emotional salience matter the most, but still much less than DeBERTa.
- In short: this fusion behaves like "DeBERTa first, tiny heuristic correction second."
